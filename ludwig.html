ABOUT

Ludwig Göransson is a Swedish composer, conductor, songwriter, and record producer. His father Tomas, a guitar teacher. He began music lessons at a young age and went on to graduate from the Stockholm Royal College of Music.
Göransson gained widespread recognition for his collaboration with filmmaker Ryan Coogler, particularly on the soundtrack for the 2018 Marvel superhero film "Black Panther." His work on the "Black Panther" score received critical acclaim and won him an Academy Award for Best Original Score.
Apart from his work on "Black Panther," Göransson has composed music for various other films, including "Creed" (2015), "Creed II" (2018), and "Tenet" (2020), directed by Christopher Nolan.
He is a prolific producer and has worked with a wide range of musical artists. He has been involved in producing and writing songs for artists. He is celebrated for his versatility as a composer and his ability to work across various musical genres, from traditional orchestral compositions to contemporary pop and hip-hop.
